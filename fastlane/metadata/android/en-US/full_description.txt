  Private Internet Access (PIA) is an open-source VPN client for Android.
  It encrypts your internet traffic and masks your IP address, helping protect
  your privacy on public Wi-Fi and other networks.

  Features include:

  * WireGuard and OpenVPN protocol support
  * Kill switch to block traffic if the VPN drops
  * Split tunneling to choose which apps use the VPN
  * DNS-based ad and tracker blocking (PIA MACE)
  * Customizable encryption settings
  * Servers in 90+ countries

  Note: This is the open-source client app only. Using it requires a paid
  subscription to PIA's proprietary backend VPN service.
